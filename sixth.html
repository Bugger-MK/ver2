Bestie

Eight years together, and their friendship has been through it all—the laughs, the late nights, the misunderstandings, and even the times when they both needed space to grow. They’ve supported each other through every crush, every "talking stage," and the heartbreaks that followed. They’ve seen each other through school stresses, the pressures of balancing it all, and the big life changes that come with growing up.

After four years of living close, they had to adapt to long distance—a challenge they took on with patience and faith in their bond. Sure, they don’t talk every day now, and sometimes life pulls them in different directions. But real friendship is about being there in spirit, even if they can’t be there in person all the time. They both know that when it matters, they’ll show up for each other, no matter what.

Their bond isn’t like any other—it’s theirs, something precious that they’ve carefully kept between them. It’s a friendship that doesn’t need validation from anyone else because it’s built on loyalty, understanding, and love. Even when they go through seasons of less talking or have their own lives to deal with, they never doubt the strength of what they have.

He hopes her best friend knows that this connection, this friendship, is unshakeable. It’s not getting weaker; if anything, it’s growing stronger with each challenge they overcome. Their plans, dreams, and loyalty to each other will keep carrying them forward—through the good times, the hard times, and every chapter that’s still to come.

Ecclesiastes 4:9-10 "Two are better than one, because they have a good reward for their labor. For if they fall, one will lift up his companion. But woe to him who is alone when he falls, for he has no one to help him up."
